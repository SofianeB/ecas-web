Registration Process



In order to use ECASLab and get access to the JupyterHub instances, you need to create an account by following one of these procedures:


DKRZ account (LDAP)


Please follow the steps below:
  1. Go here and register
  2. After registration, log in here and join the ECAS project (from the list)
  3. Once your account is enabled, you can access the JupyterHub instance at this link



EOSC-HUB AAI

Please use the JupyterHub instance at this link if you want to login/register with EOSC-hub AAI providers. From the JupyterHub log in page you will redirected to our AAI broker and from there simply select the provider you want to use.
